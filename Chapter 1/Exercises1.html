EASY DESI CHOCOLATE CAKE

People seeking a warm chocolate-flavored cake enhanced by natural jaggery sweetness will find this recipe ideal. The cake contains whole wheat flour together with jaggery and yogurt which results in a nutritious taste and soft texture. The preparation process is simple since it requires only mixing ingredients then baking before you can enjoy the final product.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups jaggery powder (or brown sugar) – for that natural caramel-like sweetness.
  • 1 ½ cups whole wheat flour – gives a slightly nutty flavor and a wholesome texture.
  • ½ cup cocoa powder – the essential component for maximum chocolate intensity.
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder – helps the cake rise beautifully.
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda – works with yogurt for an exceptional soft texture.
  • ½ teaspoon cardamom powder – adds a subtle, warm spice that pairs wonderfully with chocolate.
  • ½ teaspoon salt – enhances all the flavors.
  • 2 eggs – makes the cake soft and fluffy.
  • 1 cup yogurt – adds moisture and a slight tang to balance sweetness.
  • ½ cup ghee (or vegetable oil) – creates a rich buttery taste.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract – brings a fragrant scent and intensifies the chocolate flavor.
  • 1 cup hot milk – produces a well-blended batter with smooth consistency.

How to Make It

  1. Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease your chosen cake pan to prevent sticking.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the jaggery, flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, cardamom, and salt. Stir until well combined.
  3. Add the eggs, yogurt, ghee (or oil), and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until you obtain a uniform, dense batter.
  4. Gradually add the hot milk while stirring. The heat helps dissolve the jaggery and creates a smooth batter.
  5. Pour the batter into the greased cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes. Check for doneness with a toothpick; it should come out clean.
  6. Let the cake cool completely before slicing. This helps it set properly and makes cutting easier.
  7. Enjoy! You can eat it as is or sprinkle some powdered sugar on top. For an extra treat, drizzle some melted chocolate over it!
