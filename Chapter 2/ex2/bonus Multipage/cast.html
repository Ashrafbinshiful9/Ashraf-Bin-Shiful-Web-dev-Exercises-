Cast & Creative Team

  • Owen Wilson as Lightning McQueen - Captures McQueen's cocky yet charming personality
  • Paul Newman as Doc Hudson - A wise and mysterious mentor figure
  • Bonnie Hunt as Sally Carrera - A kind Porsche who believes in Radiator Springs
  • Larry the Cable Guy as Mater - The lovable tow truck with a heart of gold
  • Cheech Marin as Ramone - A cool lowrider and custom paint artist
  • Tony Shalhoub as Luigi - The enthusiastic Italian tire shop owner
  • Michael Wallis as Sheriff - Keeps the peace in Radiator Springs
  • Jenifer Lewis as Flo - Owner of the local diner, Flo’s V8 Café
  • Michael Keaton as Chick Hicks - The main rival and antagonist
  • Richard Petty as The King - A veteran racer, respectful and dignified
