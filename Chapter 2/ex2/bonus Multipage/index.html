Cars (2006) - Overview

"Cars" is a high-octane animated film by Pixar Animation Studios, released in 2006 by Walt Disney Pictures. Set in a world populated entirely by anthropomorphic vehicles, the story follows Lightning McQueen, a rookie race car determined to win the coveted Piston Cup. On the way to a crucial race in California, he gets lost and ends up in the forgotten town of Radiator Springs. Through his interactions with its quirky residents, McQueen learns important life lessons about friendship, humility, and what truly matters in life.

The film combines humor, emotional depth, and visually stunning animation, appealing to both kids and adults. It was a box office success and led to two sequels and multiple spin-offs.
